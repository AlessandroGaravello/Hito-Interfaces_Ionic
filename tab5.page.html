Armor Modifications

Armor Mods are found all throughout the game, sometimes dropping off enemies, or being rewarded from Treasure.

Mods can be placed into armor to improve its stats, or even give it a stat that it didn't have before. Once inserted into a piece of armor, a Mod cannot be removed unless you have a specific talent from the skill tree.

There are three tiers of Mods: Green, Blue, and Purple. Purple is the best kind, but also the rarest. As you get later into the game, the chance of finding a better mod increases.

Shield-Weaver

This is a set of legendary armor. To unlock it, you will need to find all of the Power Cell Locations. This armor, once unlocked, gives you a massive damage resistance bonus.

The Shield-Weaver has no modification slots but does possess a rechargeable shield that protects Aloy from a substantial amount of damage.

Players will know the shield is running out when it begins blinking red and making a high-pitched alarm sound.

It can be found at the end of the side quest Ancient Armory, which requires players to locate five Power Cells around the world throughout the campaign.
